GoHighLevel automation strategy

The 7-Touch System keeps Svelta top-of-mind without manual effort—while still feeling personal.

SMS + Email + Calls • Centralized
Touch 1 (Day 0)
Warm Welcome
"Hello {{contact.first_name}} ✨ My name is {{user.first_name}}... We offer customized plans, flexible financing (up to 18 mo)... I’m available today — what time works best for a quick call?"
Goal: Start conversation. Book consultation.
Touch 2 (Day 1)
Soft Follow-Up
"Hi {{contact.first_name}} ✨ I understand reviewing costs can feel overwhelming. My role is to make everything clear and manageable..."
Psychology: Normalize hesitation.
Touch 3 (Day 3)
Value + Health
"Delaying treatment can lead to more discomfort... Whether it’s timing or finances, I’m here to help you work through it."
Psychology: Gentle urgency + health protection.
Touch 4 (Day 5)
Trust + Reassurance
"Many patients initially hesitate due to anxiety or cost. Once they move forward, they often say they wish they had done it sooner..."
Psychology: Normalize fear + emphasize relief.
Touch 5 (Day 7)
Financing Anchor
"If budget is a concern, we offer financing options... Would you like me to send you the link and review it together?"
Psychology: Remove financial barrier.
Touch 6 (Day 10)
Direct Close
"Are you: A) Ready to schedule, B) Waiting for timing, C) No longer interested? Just reply A, B, or C."
Psychology: Force clarity.
Touch 7 (Day 14)
Clean Exit
"Since I haven’t heard back, I’ll pause your inquiry for now. If you decide you’re ready, I’ll be here."
Psychology: Remove pressure + preserve relationship.
